How this works

This tool compares what you currently spend on eating out to the estimated cost of making similar meals at home. Since ingredient prices vary, it gives you a savings range instead of pretending the universe runs on one exact spaghetti number.

Why ranges are better

Home cooking costs can shift depending on store prices, portion size, protein choice, sauces, and whether you’re cooking basic or going full kitchen wizard. That’s why this tool shows lower, average, and higher savings possibilities.
